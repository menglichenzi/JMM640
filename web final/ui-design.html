Workshop Management System

Role: UI/UX Designer Duration: 3 Months

Project Description

Workshop Management System (WMS) is aiming to digitalize the

current manual handling process of testing vehicle retrofit order in R&D

workshop. This application is supposed to conduct the functions of

workshop's workload management, intelligent retrofit technician

matching, and task management, automatic notification etc.

Problems

They don't have a real-time transparency system for managers to use

Low efficient & reasonable workshop system now

The old system needs plenty of time to import

low customer satisfaction because they cannot see the process

The old system is hard for managers to use now

Goals

Flexible scale with speed and cost-efficient

Real-time Transparency

Resource utilization

Handing time 15% decrease

On-time ratio 10% increase

Better customer satisfaction

A large resource pool to support rapid ramp-up requets

Process

We met with customers twice a week to report our work and what we did during each sprint. It's common that customers change their minds and requests so we had to revise our prototype before delivery.

UI Prototype

I made a prototype according to the customer's requirements. And I used black and grey as the main colors because it fits their overall company's style and also uses their official font.

I made a prototype according to the customer's requirements. And I used black and grey as the main colors because it fits their overall company's style and also uses their official font.

I designed KANBAN for customers to use according to the real space arrangement and it benefits managers to control the condition of the workshop.

After several times meeting with technicians who will use this in the future, and it would show on their big screen for their customers and technicians. And their customers would clearly find their car's process according to the notification numbers and also this KANBAN would change each 30 seconds.

User guide

My project manager and I wrote this user guide with some pictures on it for our customers to use in the future and it had details and the correct way to use it. And also, it's good for us to do user research in the future.

Benefits

The application could significantly improve the handling efficiency, transparency, utilization, and customer satisfaction of workshops by realizing those listed functions. Besides mentioned benefits, this platform could also collect the data of workshop utilization and technicians capacity management, which provide the basement of further intelligent data-driven planning in the future.
