Stripes (UX/UI Design)

Application Redesign

Role: UI/UX Designer Duration: 3 Months

Project Description

Stripes Symptoms Tracker is a mobile app that allows the caregivers of children with autism to track and record the child's symptoms. Multiple caregivers can share an account, and one account can include multiple participants. The app allows caregivers to record symptoms, run & record tests, record lifestyle changes, and record BMI. Caregivers are also able to export symptom data as an Excel spreadsheet.

Research process

Our UX research team conducted a usability study to evaluate the usability of the Stripes Symptom Tracker, an application for tracking symptoms of people on the autism spectrum. We recruited five caregivers of special needs individuals and asked each of them to complete five tasks within the Stripes app. Our aim was to determine the app's effectiveness, efficiency, and user satisfaction using the app by observing participants as they worked through each task and interviewing them about their experiences.
